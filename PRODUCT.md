# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Senior High School students in the Philippines, specifically those aiming for university (PUP Iskolars) and those from marginalized or low-income backgrounds. They are often navigating a high-stakes, stressful transition to higher education.

## Product Purpose
ScholarAid exists to democratize access to education by simplifying the scholarship search and preparation process. It provides AI-driven matching, eligibility verification, and exam prep to move students from uncertainty to application-readiness.

## Brand Personality
Innovative, Accessible, Supportive.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, ad-heavy scholarship portals.
- Cold, impersonal, or bureaucratic government forms.
- Generic "SaaS-cream" interfaces that feel disconnected from the student reality.

## Design Principles
- **Empowerment through Clarity**: Complex eligibility rules should be distilled into clear, actionable steps.
- **Resilient Navigation**: The UI should feel stable and supportive, especially on lower-end devices or slower connections.
- **Hopeful Momentum**: Use motion and visual cues to celebrate progress (e.g., matching a scholarship, completing a practice quiz).
- **Focused Utility**: Prioritize the task at hand (finding, checking, reviewing) over decorative fluff.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Mobile-First Resilience**: Optimized for mobile usage where many students access the web.
- **High Contrast & Readability**: Ensuring clarity for all users, regardless of visual acuity or screen quality.
- **Future-Proofing**: Standard web accessibility (WCAG) as a baseline, with a focus on cognitive ease.
